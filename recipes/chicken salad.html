Sophia's Stellar Seared Chicken Salad With a Avacado Dressing

Recipe Repository: Home

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 1 tablespoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • olive oil as needed?
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • 1/2 of a red cabbage
  • 1/2 of a red onion
  • cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • salt as needed?

    • Ingredients for the Avacado Dressing

  • 1 Avacado
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 4 tablespoons of Greek yogurt
  • 3 tablespoons of olive oil
  • a handful of cilantro leaves
  • 3-4 tablespoons of water (to loosen)
  • salt and pepper as needed?

Instructions

  1. Pound the chicken breast into an even thickness using a mallet.
  2. Season the chicken with salt, pepper, paprika, onion powder and garlic powder on both sides.
  3. Heat a pan with olive oil on medium heat. Place the chicken breast smooth side down and cook for 6-8 minutes until golden brown.
  4. Bring down the heat to low, flip the chicken and cook on the other side with a closed lid for 10 minutes
  5. Turn off the heat and let it rest in the pan for 10 minutes before slicing.
  6. Blend together avocado, lemon juice, garlic, Greek yogurt, olive oil, cilantro, salt and pepper until smooth. Add water as needed while blending to loosen/thin the dressing to the preferred consistency.
  7. slice the chicken, lettuce, red onion, red cabbage and the cherry tomatoes. Toss in the avacado dressing and serve immediatley.
